Synapse is **source-available**, not open source. The public license is the
[PolyForm Noncommercial License 1.0.0](LICENSE.md), which permits **noncommercial
use only**.

You need a **paid commercial license** if you fall into any of these categories:

- You use Synapse inside a **for-profit company or organization** (including
  internal tooling, automation, evaluation that informs a commercial decision,
  or any use "in the course of business").
- You **build a product or service** — commercial, hosted, embedded, or
  distributed — that includes, depends on, links against, or is powered by
  Synapse or a derivative of it.
- You **resell, sublicense, or redistribute** Synapse or a derivative as part of
  a paid offering.
- You provide **paid services** (consulting, integration, support, managed
  hosting) built around Synapse.

You do **not** need a commercial license for genuinely noncommercial use:
personal projects, hobby use, private study and experimentation, academic
research, teaching, or use by registered nonprofits and government bodies — as
described in the [LICENSE](LICENSE.md). When in doubt, ask.

## How to get a commercial license

Email **chrisroyseai@gmail.com** with:

1. Your company / organization name and website.
2. A short description of how you intend to use Synapse (internal use, embedding
   in a product, hosting as a service, etc.).
3. Rough scale (team size, number of deployments or seats, or product
   distribution volume).

You'll get back pricing and terms. Commercial licenses can cover internal
business use, embedding/redistribution, OEM arrangements, and support, and can
be tailored to your deployment.

## Contributions

By contributing to this repository you agree to the terms in
[CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md), which let the maintainer offer your
contribution under both the noncommercial license and commercial licenses. This
is what keeps dual licensing possible.
